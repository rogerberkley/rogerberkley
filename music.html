Poke to release a new music track

Let's try this!

What is a Poke?

A Poke is a virtual gesture that signals for the release of one of my new music tracks.

How is a Poke performed?

A Poke is performed through a digital transaction.

What is the purpose of a Poke?

The purpose of a Poke is to signal that a specified upcoming musical release is confirmed for release.

What is a Poke Window?

A Poke Window is a timeframe within which a pending musical release is available as accepting Pokes.

What is Poke Value?

Poke Value is an amount that is presented with each available Poke and it is used as minimum value accepted to mark the Poke as Valid if the Poke's transaction value matches or exceeds the Poke Value.

What happens when a Poke's transaction is confirmed and the Poke's transaction amount matches the Poke Value?

If the confirmation occurs during the active Poke Window and the Poke's transaction value matches the Poke Value then the track release will be marked as confirmed and the music track will be released to music platforms.

What happens when a Poke's transaction is confirmed and the Poke's transaction amount is less than the Poke Value?

When a Poke's transaction amount is less than the Poke Value the transaction is ignored and has no effect on the available Poke.

Am I purchasing anything when I make a Poke?

No, you are not purchasing anything other than the opportunity to brag anytime for being the person behind digitally gesturing for the release of a musical track to music platforms.

If I fulfil a Poke, do I earn any rights to the music track?

No, you do not get any rights to a released music track or any other rights. All rights remain reserved to the original creator.

What happens if an available Poke is not fulfilled within the Poke Window?

The poke expires and becomes unavailable, and the track is not released by the end of the Poke Window. The track may be released any other time after the end of the Poke Window with or without another Poke.
